# Psalm 133
##### <span class=arrow-left></span>[[ps132|Prev]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[ps133notes|Notes]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[ps134|Next]]<span class=arrow-right></span>
###### 1
><span class=verse-first-poetry>1</span>Look! How good and how pleasant it is
>When brothers truly live in unity.
###### 2
><span class=verse-body-poetry>2</span>It is like fine oil poured on the head,
>Which flows down the beard—
>Aaron’s beard,
>And then flows down his garments.
###### 3
><span class=verse-body-poetry>3</span>It is like the dew of Hermon,
>Which flows down upon the hills of Zion.
>Indeed, that is where the Lᴏʀᴅ has decreed
>A blessing will be available—eternal life.
