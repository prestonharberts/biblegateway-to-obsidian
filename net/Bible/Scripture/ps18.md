# Psalm 18
##### <span class=arrow-left></span>[[ps17|Prev]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[ps18notes|Notes]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[ps19|Next]]<span class=arrow-right></span>
###### 1
><span class=verse-first-poetry>1</span>He said:
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I love you, Lᴏʀᴅ, my source of strength!
###### 2
><span class=verse-body-poetry>2</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ is my high ridge, my stronghold, my deliverer.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My God is my rocky summit where I take shelter,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My shield, the horn that saves me, and my refuge.
###### 3
><span class=verse-body-poetry>3</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I called to the Lᴏʀᴅ, who is worthy of praise,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>And I was delivered from my enemies.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 4
><span class=verse-first-poetry>4</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The waves of death engulfed me,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The currents of chaos overwhelmed me.
###### 5
><span class=verse-body-poetry>5</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The ropes of Sheol tightened around me,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The snares of death trapped me.
###### 6
><span class=verse-body-poetry>6</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>In my distress I called to the Lᴏʀᴅ;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I cried out to my God.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>From his heavenly temple he heard my voice;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He listened to my cry for help.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 7
><span class=verse-first-poetry>7</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The earth heaved and shook.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The roots of the mountains trembled;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They heaved because he was angry.
###### 8
><span class=verse-body-poetry>8</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Smoke ascended from his nose;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Fire devoured as it came from his mouth.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He hurled down fiery coals.
###### 9
><span class=verse-body-poetry>9</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He made the sky sink as he descended;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>A thick cloud was under his feet.
###### 10
><span class=verse-body-poetry>10</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He mounted a winged angel and flew;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He glided on the wings of the wind.
###### 11
><span class=verse-body-poetry>11</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He shrouded himself in darkness,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>In thick rain clouds.
###### 12
><span class=verse-body-poetry>12</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>From the brightness in front of him came
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Hail and fiery coals.
###### 13
><span class=verse-body-poetry>13</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ thundered in the sky;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Most High shouted.
###### 14
><span class=verse-body-poetry>14</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He shot his arrows and scattered them,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Many lightning bolts and routed them.
###### 15
><span class=verse-body-poetry>15</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The depths of the sea were exposed;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The inner regions of the world were uncovered
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>By your battle cry, Lᴏʀᴅ,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>By the powerful breath from your nose.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 16
><span class=verse-first-poetry>16</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He reached down from above and took hold of me;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He pulled me from the surging water.
###### 17
><span class=verse-body-poetry>17</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He rescued me from my strong enemy,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>From those who hate me,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>For they were too strong for me.
###### 18
><span class=verse-body-poetry>18</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They confronted me in my day of calamity,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>But the Lᴏʀᴅ helped me.
###### 19
><span class=verse-body-poetry>19</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He brought me out into a wide open place;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He delivered me because he was pleased with me.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 20
><span class=verse-first-poetry>20</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ repaid me for my godly deeds;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He rewarded my blameless behavior.
###### 21
><span class=verse-body-poetry>21</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>For I have obeyed the Lᴏʀᴅ’s commands;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I have not rebelled against my God.
###### 22
><span class=verse-body-poetry>22</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>For I am aware of all his regulations,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>And I do not reject his rules.
###### 23
><span class=verse-body-poetry>23</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I was innocent before him,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>And kept myself from sinning.
###### 24
><span class=verse-body-poetry>24</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ rewarded me for my godly deeds;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He took notice of my blameless behavior.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 25
><span class=verse-first-poetry>25</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You prove to be loyal to one who is faithful;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You prove to be trustworthy to one who is innocent.
###### 26
><span class=verse-body-poetry>26</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You prove to be reliable to one who is blameless,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>But you prove to be deceptive to one who is perverse.
###### 27
><span class=verse-body-poetry>27</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>For you deliver oppressed people,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>But you bring down those who have a proud look.
###### 28
><span class=verse-body-poetry>28</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Indeed, you light my lamp, Lᴏʀᴅ.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My God illuminates the darkness around me.
###### 29
><span class=verse-body-poetry>29</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Indeed, with your help I can charge against an army;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>By my God’s power I can jump over a wall.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 30
><span class=verse-first-poetry>30</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The one true God acts in a faithful manner;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ’s promise is reliable.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He is a shield to all who take shelter in him.
###### 31
><span class=verse-body-poetry>31</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Indeed, who is God besides the Lᴏʀᴅ?
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Who is a protector besides our God?
###### 32
><span class=verse-body-poetry>32</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The one true God gives me strength;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He removes the obstacles in my way.
###### 33
><span class=verse-body-poetry>33</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He gives me the agility of a deer;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He enables me to negotiate the rugged terrain.
###### 34
><span class=verse-body-poetry>34</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He trains my hands for battle;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My arms can bend even the strongest bow.
###### 35
><span class=verse-body-poetry>35</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You give me your protective shield;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Your right hand supports me.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Your willingness to help enables me to prevail.
###### 36
><span class=verse-body-poetry>36</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You widen my path;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My feet do not slip.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 37
><span class=verse-first-poetry>37</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I chase my enemies and catch them;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I do not turn back until I wipe them out.
###### 38
><span class=verse-body-poetry>38</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I beat them to death;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They fall at my feet.
###### 39
><span class=verse-body-poetry>39</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You give me strength for battle;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You make my foes kneel before me.
###### 40
><span class=verse-body-poetry>40</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You make my enemies retreat;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I destroy those who hate me.
###### 41
><span class=verse-body-poetry>41</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They cry out, but there is no one to help them;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They cry out to the Lᴏʀᴅ, but he does not answer them.
###### 42
><span class=verse-body-poetry>42</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I grind them as fine windblown dust;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I beat them underfoot like clay in the streets.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 43
><span class=verse-first-poetry>43</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You rescue me from a hostile army.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You make me a leader of nations;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>People over whom I had no authority are now my subjects.
###### 44
><span class=verse-body-poetry>44</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>When they hear of my exploits, they submit to me.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Foreigners are powerless before me.
###### 45
><span class=verse-body-poetry>45</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>Foreigners lose their courage;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>They shake with fear as they leave their strongholds.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 46
><span class=verse-first-poetry>46</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The Lᴏʀᴅ is alive!
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>My Protector is praiseworthy.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The God who delivers me is exalted as king.
###### 47
><span class=verse-body-poetry>47</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>The one true God completely vindicates me;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He makes nations submit to me.
###### 48
><span class=verse-body-poetry>48</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He delivers me from my enemies.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You snatch me away from those who attack me;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>You rescue me from violent men.
###### 49
><span class=verse-body-poetry>49</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>So I will give you thanks before the nations, O Lᴏʀᴅ.
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>I will sing praises to you.
###### 50
><span class=verse-body-poetry>50</span><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He gives his king magnificent victories;
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>He is faithful to his chosen ruler,
><span class=poetry-quote-double>“</span>To David and his descendants forever.”
