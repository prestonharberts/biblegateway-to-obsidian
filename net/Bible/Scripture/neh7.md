# Nehemiah 7

###### 1
<span class=verse-first>1</span>When the wall had been rebuilt and I had positioned the doors, and the gatekeepers, the singers, and the Levites had been appointed,
###### 2
<span class=verse-body>2</span>I then put in charge over Jerusalem my brother Hanani and Hananiah the chief of the citadel, for he was a faithful man and feared God more than many do.
###### 3
<span class=verse-body>3</span>I said to them, “The gates of Jerusalem must not be opened in the early morning, until those who are standing guard close the doors and lock them. Position residents of Jerusalem as guards, some at their guard stations and some near their homes.”
###### 4
<span class=verse-body>4</span>Now the city was spread out and large, and there were not a lot of people in it. At that time houses had not been rebuilt.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 5
<span class=verse-first>5</span>My God placed it on my heart to gather the leaders, the officials, and the ordinary people so they could be enrolled on the basis of genealogy. I found the genealogical records of those who had formerly returned. Here is what I found written in that record:
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 6
<span class=verse-first>6</span>These are the people of the province who returned from the captivity of the exiles, whom King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon had forced into exile. They returned to Jerusalem and to Judah, each to his own city.

###### 64
<span class=verse-body>64</span>They searched for their records in the genealogical materials, but none were found. They were therefore excluded from the priesthood.
###### 65
<span class=verse-body>65</span>The governor instructed them not to eat any of the sacred food until there was a priest who could consult the Urim and Thummim.

###### 66
<span class=verse-first>66</span>The entire group numbered 42,360—
###### 67
<span class=verse-body>67</span>not counting their 7,337 male and female servants. They also had 245 male and female singers.
###### 68
<span class=verse-body>68</span>They had 736 horses, 245 mules,
###### 69
<span class=verse-body>69</span> 435 camels, and 6,720 donkeys.
###### 70
<span class=verse-first>70</span>Some of the family leaders contributed to the work. The governor contributed to the treasury 1,000 gold drachmas, 50 bowls, and 530 priestly garments.
###### 71
<span class=verse-body>71</span>Some of the family leaders gave to the project treasury 20,000 gold drachmas and 2,200 silver minas.
###### 72
<span class=verse-body>72</span>What the rest of the people gave amounted to 20,000 gold drachmas, 2,000 silver minas, and 67 priestly garments.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 73
<span class=verse-first>73</span>The priests, the Levites, the gatekeepers, the singers, some of the people, the temple servants, and all the rest of Israel lived in their cities.
