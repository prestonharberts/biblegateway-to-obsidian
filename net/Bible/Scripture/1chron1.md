# 1 Chronicles 1
##### <span class=arrow-left></span>[[2kings25|Prev]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron1notes|Notes]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron2|Next]]<span class=arrow-right></span>
## Adam’s Descendants
###### 1
<span class=verse-first>1</span>Adam, Seth, Enosh,
###### 2
<span class=verse-body>2</span>Kenan, Mahalalel, Jered,
###### 3
<span class=verse-body>3</span>Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech,
###### 4
<span class=verse-body>4</span>Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
## Japheth’s Descendants
###### 5
<span class=verse-first>5</span>The sons of Japheth: Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras.
###### 6
<span class=verse-body>6</span>The sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz, Riphath, and Togarmah.
###### 7
<span class=verse-body>7</span>The sons of Javan: Elishah, Tarshish, the Kittites, and the Rodanites.
## Ham’s Descendants
###### 8
<span class=verse-first>8</span>The sons of Ham: Cush, Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.
###### 9
<span class=verse-body>9</span>The sons of Cush: Seba, Havilah, Sabta, Raamah, and Sabteca.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Raamah: Sheba and Dedan.
###### 10
<span class=verse-body>10</span>Cush was the father of Nimrod, who established himself as a mighty warrior on earth.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 11
<span class=verse-first>11</span>Mizraim was the father of the Ludites, Anamites, Lehabites, Naphtuhites,
###### 12
<span class=verse-body>12</span>Pathrusites, Casluhites (from whom the Philistines descended), and the Caphtorites.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 13
<span class=verse-first>13</span>Canaan was the father of Sidon—his firstborn—and Heth,
###### 14
<span class=verse-body>14</span>as well as the Jebusites, Amorites, Girgashites,
###### 15
<span class=verse-body>15</span>Hivites, Arkites, Sinites,
###### 16
<span class=verse-body>16</span>Arvadites, Zemarites, and Hamathites.
## Shem’s Descendants
###### 17
<span class=verse-first>17</span>The sons of Shem: Elam, Asshur, Arphaxad, Lud, and Aram.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Aram: Uz, Hul, Gether, and Meshech.
###### 18
<span class=verse-body>18</span>Arphaxad was the father of Shelah, and Shelah was the father of Eber.
###### 19
<span class=verse-body>19</span>Two sons were born to Eber: the first was named Peleg, for during his lifetime the earth was divided; his brother’s name was Joktan.
###### 20
<span class=verse-body>20</span>Joktan was the father of Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah,
###### 21
<span class=verse-body>21</span>Hadoram, Uzal, Diklah,
###### 22
<span class=verse-body>22</span>Ebal, Abimael, Sheba,
###### 23
<span class=verse-body>23</span>Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 24
<span class=verse-first>24</span>Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,
###### 25
<span class=verse-body>25</span>Eber, Peleg, Reu,
###### 26
<span class=verse-body>26</span>Serug, Nahor, Terah,
###### 27
<span class=verse-body>27</span>Abram (that is, Abraham).
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 28
<span class=verse-first>28</span>The sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael.
###### 29
<span class=verse-body>29</span>These were their descendants:
## Ishmael’s Descendants
Ishmael’s firstborn son was Nebaioth; the others were Kedar, Adbeel, Mibsam,
###### 30
<span class=verse-body>30</span>Mishma, Dumah, Massa, Hadad, Tema,
###### 31
<span class=verse-body>31</span>Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These were the sons of Ishmael.
## Keturah’s Descendants
###### 32
<span class=verse-body>32</span>The sons to whom Keturah, Abraham’s concubine, gave birth: Zimran, Jokshan, Medan, Midian, Ishbak, Shuah.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Jokshan: Sheba and Dedan.
###### 33
<span class=verse-body>33</span>The sons of Midian: Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, Abida, and Eldaah. All these were the sons of Keturah.
## Isaac’s Descendants
###### 34
<span class=verse-body>34</span>Abraham was the father of Isaac. The sons of Isaac: Esau and Israel.
## Esau’s Descendants
###### 35
<span class=verse-body>35</span>The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, Jeush, Jalam, and Korah.
###### 36
<span class=verse-body>36</span>The sons of Eliphaz: Teman, Omar, Zephi, Gatam, Kenaz, and (by Timna) Amalek.
###### 37
<span class=verse-body>37</span>The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.
## The Descendants of Seir
###### 38
<span class=verse-body>38</span>The sons of Seir: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah, Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan.
###### 39
<span class=verse-body>39</span>The sons of Lotan: Hori and Homam. (Timna was Lotan’s sister.)
###### 40
<span class=verse-body>40</span>The sons of Shobal: Alyan, Manahath, Ebal, Shephi, and Onam.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah.
###### 41
<span class=verse-body>41</span>The son of Anah: Dishon.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Dishon: Hamran, Eshban, Ithran, and Keran.
###### 42
<span class=verse-body>42</span>The sons of Ezer: Bilhan, Zaavan, Jaakan.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Dishan: Uz and Aran.
## Kings of Edom
###### 43
<span class=verse-first>43</span>These were the kings who reigned in the land of Edom before any king ruled over the Israelites: Bela son of Beor; the name of his city was Dinhabah.
###### 44
<span class=verse-body>44</span>When Bela died, Jobab son of Zerah from Bozrah, succeeded him.
###### 45
<span class=verse-body>45</span>When Jobab died, Husham from the land of the Temanites succeeded him.
###### 46
<span class=verse-body>46</span>When Husham died, Hadad son of Bedad succeeded him. He struck down the Midianites in the plains of Moab; the name of his city was Avith.
###### 47
<span class=verse-body>47</span>When Hadad died, Samlah from Masrekah succeeded him.
###### 48
<span class=verse-body>48</span>When Samlah died, Shaul from Rehoboth on the River succeeded him.
###### 49
<span class=verse-body>49</span>When Shaul died, Baal Hanan son of Achbor succeeded him.
###### 50
<span class=verse-body>50</span>When Baal Hanan died, Hadad succeeded him; the name of his city was Pai. His wife was Mehetabel, daughter of Matred, daughter of Me-Zahab.
###### 51
<span class=verse-body>51</span>Hadad died.
## Tribal Chiefs of Edom
The tribal chiefs of Edom were: Timna, Alvah, Jetheth,
###### 52
<span class=verse-body>52</span>Oholibamah, Elah, Pinon,
###### 53
<span class=verse-body>53</span>Kenaz, Teman, Mibzar,
###### 54
<span class=verse-body>54</span>Magdiel, and Iram. These were the tribal chiefs of Edom.
