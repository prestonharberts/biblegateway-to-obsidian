# 1 Chronicles 8
##### <span class=arrow-left></span>[[1chron7|Prev]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron8notes|Notes]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron9|Next]]<span class=arrow-right></span>
## Benjamin’s Descendants (Continued)
###### 1
<span class=verse-first>1</span>Benjamin was the father of Bela, his firstborn; Ashbel was born second, Aharah third,
###### 2
<span class=verse-body>2</span>Nohah fourth, and Rapha fifth.
###### 3
<span class=verse-body>3</span>Bela’s sons were Addar, Gera, Abihud,
###### 4
<span class=verse-body>4</span>Abishua, Naaman, Ahoah,
###### 5
<span class=verse-body>5</span>Gera, Shephuphan, and Huram.
###### 6
<span class=verse-body>6</span>These were the descendants of Ehud who were leaders of the families living in Geba who were forced to move to Manahath:
###### 7
<span class=verse-body>7</span>Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera, who moved them. Gera was the father of Uzzah and Ahihud.
###### 8
<span class=verse-body>8</span>Shaharaim fathered sons in Moab after he divorced his wives Hushim and Baara.
###### 9
<span class=verse-body>9</span>By his wife Hodesh he fathered Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malkam,
###### 10
<span class=verse-body>10</span>Jeuz, Sakia, and Mirmah. These were his sons; they were family leaders.
###### 11
<span class=verse-body>11</span>By Hushim he fathered Abitub and Elpaal.
###### 12
<span class=verse-body>12</span>The sons of Elpaal: Eber, Misham, Shemed (who built Ono and Lod, as well as its surrounding towns),
###### 13
<span class=verse-body>13</span>Beriah, and Shema. They were leaders of the families living in Aijalon and chased out the inhabitants of Gath.
###### 14
<span class=verse-body>14</span>Ahio, Shashak, Jeremoth,
###### 15
<span class=verse-body>15</span>Zebadiah, Arad, Eder,
###### 16
<span class=verse-body>16</span>Michael, Ishpah, and Joha were the sons of Beriah.
###### 17
<span class=verse-body>17</span>Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber,
###### 18
<span class=verse-body>18</span>Ishmerai, Izliah, and Jobab were the sons of Elpaal.
###### 19
<span class=verse-body>19</span>Jakim, Zikri, Zabdi,
###### 20
<span class=verse-body>20</span>Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel,
###### 21
<span class=verse-body>21</span>Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were the sons of Shimei.
###### 22
<span class=verse-body>22</span>Ishpan, Eber, Eliel,
###### 23
<span class=verse-body>23</span>Abdon, Zikri, Hanan,
###### 24
<span class=verse-body>24</span>Hananiah, Elam, Anthothijah,
###### 25
<span class=verse-body>25</span>Iphdeiah, and Penuel were the sons of Shashak.
###### 26
<span class=verse-body>26</span>Shamsherai, Shechariah, Athaliah,
###### 27
<span class=verse-body>27</span>Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zikri were the sons of Jeroham.
###### 28
<span class=verse-body>28</span>These were the family leaders listed in the genealogical records; they lived in Jerusalem.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

###### 29
<span class=verse-first>29</span>The father of Gibeon lived in Gibeon; his wife’s name was Maacah.
###### 30
<span class=verse-body>30</span>His firstborn son was Abdon, followed by Zur, Kish, Baal, Nadab,
###### 31
<span class=verse-body>31</span>Gedor, Ahio, Zeker, and Mikloth.
###### 32
<span class=verse-body>32</span>Mikloth was the father of Shimeah. They also lived near their relatives in Jerusalem.
###### 33
<span class=verse-body>33</span>Ner was the father of Kish, and Kish was the father of Saul. Saul was the father of Jonathan, Malki-Shua, Abinadab, and Eshbaal.
###### 34
<span class=verse-body>34</span>The son of Jonathan: Meribbaal.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

Meribbaal was the father of Micah.
###### 35
<span class=verse-body>35</span>The sons of Micah: Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz.
###### 36
<span class=verse-body>36</span>Ahaz was the father of Jehoaddah, and Jehoaddah was the father of Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri. Zimri was the father of Moza,
###### 37
<span class=verse-body>37</span>and Moza was the father of Binea. His son was Raphah, whose son was Eleasah, whose son was Azel.
###### 38
<span class=verse-body>38</span>Azel had six sons: Azrikam his firstborn, followed by Ishmael, Sheariah, Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.
###### 39
<span class=verse-body>39</span>The sons of his brother Eshek:
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

Ulam was his firstborn, Jeush second, and Eliphelet third.
###### 40
<span class=verse-body>40</span>The sons of Ulam were warriors who were adept archers. They had many sons and grandsons, a total of 150.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

All these were the descendants of Benjamin.
