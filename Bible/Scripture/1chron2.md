# 1 Chronicles 2
##### <span class=arrow-left></span>[[1chron1|Prev]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron2notes|Notes]]<span class=navigation-separator></span>[[1chron3|Next]]<span class=arrow-right></span>
## Israel’s Descendants
###### 1
<span class=verse-first>1</span>These were the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah; Issachar and Zebulun;
###### 2
<span class=verse-body>2</span>Dan, Joseph, and Benjamin;
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.
## Judah’s Descendants
###### 3
<span class=verse-first>3</span>The sons of Judah: Er, Onan, and Shelah. These three were born to him by Bathshua, a Canaanite woman. Er, Judah’s firstborn, displeased the Lᴏʀᴅ, so the Lᴏʀᴅ killed him.
###### 4
<span class=verse-body>4</span>Tamar, Judah’s daughter-in-law, bore to him Perez and Zerah. Judah had five sons in all.
###### 5
<span class=verse-body>5</span>The sons of Perez: Hezron and Hamul.
###### 6
<span class=verse-body>6</span>The sons of Zerah: Zimri, Ethan, Heman, Kalkol, Dara—five in all.
###### 7
<span class=verse-body>7</span>The son of Carmi: Achan, who brought the disaster on Israel when he stole what was devoted to God.
###### 8
<span class=verse-body>8</span>The son of Ethan: Azariah.
###### 9
<span class=verse-body>9</span>The sons born to Hezron: Jerahmeel, Ram, and Caleb.
## Ram’s Descendants
###### 10
<span class=verse-body>10</span>Ram was the father of Amminadab, and Amminadab was the father of Nahshon, the tribal chief of Judah.
###### 11
<span class=verse-body>11</span>Nahshon was the father of Salma, and Salma was the father of Boaz.
###### 12
<span class=verse-body>12</span>Boaz was the father of Obed, and Obed was the father of Jesse.
###### 13
<span class=verse-body>13</span>Jesse was the father of Eliab, his firstborn; Abinadab was born second, Shimea third,
###### 14
<span class=verse-body>14</span>Nethanel fourth, Raddai fifth,
###### 15
<span class=verse-body>15</span>Ozem sixth, and David seventh.
###### 16
<span class=verse-body>16</span>Their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail. Zeruiah’s three sons were Abshai, Joab, and Asahel.
###### 17
<span class=verse-body>17</span>Abigail bore Amasa, whose father was Jether the Ishmaelite.
## Caleb’s Descendants
###### 18
<span class=verse-first>18</span>Caleb son of Hezron fathered sons by his wife Azubah (also known as Jerioth). Her sons were Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon.
###### 19
<span class=verse-body>19</span>When Azubah died, Caleb married Ephrath, who bore him Hur.
###### 20
<span class=verse-body>20</span>Hur was the father of Uri, and Uri was the father of Bezalel.
###### 21
<span class=verse-body>21</span>Later Hezron slept with the daughter of Makir, the father of Gilead. (He had married her when he was sixty years old.) She bore him Segub.
###### 22
<span class=verse-body>22</span>Segub was the father of Jair, who owned twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead.
###### 23
<span class=verse-body>23</span>(Geshur and Aram captured the towns of Jair, along with Kenath and its sixty surrounding towns.) All these were descendants of Makir, the father of Gilead.
###### 24
<span class=verse-body>24</span>After Hezron’s death, Caleb slept with Ephrath, his father Hezron’s widow, and she bore to him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.
## Jerahmeel’s Descendants
###### 25
<span class=verse-first>25</span>The sons of Jerahmeel, Hezron’s firstborn, were Ram, the firstborn, Bunah, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah.
###### 26
<span class=verse-body>26</span>Jerahmeel had another wife named Atarah; she was Onam’s mother.
###### 27
<span class=verse-body>27</span>The sons of Ram, Jerahmeel’s firstborn, were Maaz, Jamin, and Eker.
###### 28
<span class=verse-body>28</span>The sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Shammai: Nadab and Abishur.
###### 29
<span class=verse-body>29</span>Abishur’s wife was Abihail, who bore him Ahban and Molid.
###### 30
<span class=verse-body>30</span>The sons of Nadab: Seled and Appaim. (Seled died without having sons.)
###### 31
<span class=verse-body>31</span>The son of Appaim: Ishi.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The son of Ishi: Sheshan.
The son of Sheshan: Ahlai.
###### 32
<span class=verse-body>32</span>The sons of Jada, Shammai’s brother: Jether and Jonathan. (Jether died without having sons.)
###### 33
<span class=verse-body>33</span>The sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

These were the descendants of Jerahmeel.
###### 34
<span class=verse-body>34</span>Sheshan had no sons, only daughters. Sheshan had an Egyptian servant named Jarha.
###### 35
<span class=verse-body>35</span>Sheshan gave his daughter to his servant Jarha as a wife; she bore him Attai.
###### 36
<span class=verse-body>36</span>Attai was the father of Nathan, and Nathan was the father of Zabad.
###### 37
<span class=verse-body>37</span>Zabad was the father of Ephlal, and Ephlal was the father of Obed.
###### 38
<span class=verse-body>38</span>Obed was the father of Jehu, and Jehu was the father of Azariah.
###### 39
<span class=verse-body>39</span>Azariah was the father of Helez, and Helez was the father of Eleasah.
###### 40
<span class=verse-body>40</span>Eleasah was the father of Sismai, and Sismai was the father of Shallum.
###### 41
<span class=verse-body>41</span>Shallum was the father of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah was the father of Elishama.
## More of Caleb’s Descendants
###### 42
<span class=verse-body>42</span>The sons of Caleb, Jerahmeel’s brother: His firstborn Mesha, the father of Ziph, and his second son Mareshah, the father of Hebron.
###### 43
<span class=verse-body>43</span>The sons of Hebron: Korah, Tappuah, Rekem, and Shema.
###### 44
<span class=verse-body>44</span>Shema was the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam. Rekem was the father of Shammai.
###### 45
<span class=verse-body>45</span>Shammai’s son was Maon, who was the father of Beth Zur.
###### 46
<span class=verse-body>46</span>Caleb’s concubine Ephah bore Haran, Moza, and Gazez. Haran was the father of Gazez.
###### 47
<span class=verse-body>47</span>The sons of Jahdai: Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah, and Shaaph.
###### 48
<span class=verse-body>48</span>Caleb’s concubine Maacah bore Sheber and Tirhanah.
###### 49
<span class=verse-body>49</span>She also bore Shaaph the father of Madmannah and Sheva the father of Machbenah and Gibea. Caleb’s daughter was Achsah.
###### 50
<span class=verse-body>50</span>These were the descendants of Caleb.
<div class=paragraph-break></div>

The sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrath: Shobal, the father of Kiriath Jearim,
###### 51
<span class=verse-body>51</span>Salma, the father of Bethlehem, and Hareph, the father of Beth Gader.
###### 52
<span class=verse-body>52</span>The sons of Shobal, the father of Kiriath Jearim, were Haroeh, half the Manahathites,
###### 53
<span class=verse-body>53</span>the clans of Kiriath Jearim—the Ithrites, Puthites, Shumathites, and Mishraites. (The Zorathites and Eshtaolites descended from these groups.)
###### 54
<span class=verse-body>54</span>The sons of Salma: Bethlehem, the Netophathites, Atroth Beth Joab, half the Manahathites, the Zorites,
###### 55
<span class=verse-body>55</span>and the clans of the scribes who lived in Jabez: the Tirathites, Shimeathites, and Sucathites. These are the Kenites who descended from Hammath, the father of Beth Rechab.
